  {flag} <red> <green> <blue> or false

    Changes result's leather armor color, colors must be 3 numbers ranged from 0 to 255, the red, green and blue channels.
    Setting it to false will reset leather color.
    
    Examples:
      {flag} 255 100 50
      {flag} false
    
    Specific items: leather armor items
    Aliases: {aliases}
